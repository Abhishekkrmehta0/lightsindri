Who We Are

LiGHT Sindri is part of the expansion initiative of GYWS (Gopali Youth Welfare Society). The aim is to form autonomous centres at various institutes and achieve the global vision and mission of the NGO around each centre.

In Detail

LiGHT Sindri was established in May 2021 only with nine members which consisted of the undergraduate students of BIT Sindri. The aim of this student run NGO is to resolve the untouched issues of the society.
